Olivia

Vocals/Guitar

Having been lucky enough to come from a long line of musicians, I’ve been able to learn how to play various instruments growing up. With the help of my Dad, I collaborated with many of my peers throughout middle and high school to form bands and learn how to work together to create musical projects and perform in numerous venues scattered throughout the Seattle area. When i first started seriously playing guitar at age 12 i had the common rite-of-passage sort of experience that most guitar players do when starting out, and learned all the classic rock basics that you hear your dad listen to while taking you on grocery runs. After learning the basics, i noticed my interest shifting to the prog-rock era of guitar, then metal, and of course the blues (tfw guitar players discover SRV), and here I am today focusing more on the intricacies of jazz-funk and psychedelia. I feel that now more than ever, my taste in music and the things i learn on guitar has opened up in a huge way for me, and I’m allowing myself to learn some beautifully calculated parts of music that often go under-appreciated especially in the guitar-playing community. I think this more open-minded attitude that I’ve been having towards music has improved the way i play and appreciate music more than I’ve ever experienced and I’m more passionate than ever to discover what else music across the globe has to teach me.
