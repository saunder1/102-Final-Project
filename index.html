bio

Live band from Bellingham, Washington. Formed out of a shared appreciation for artists like Duster, Ceras, and Deerhunter, Lint is a collaborative project between its four members Benjamin Alexander, Olivia Anderson, Connor Cedergreen and Avery Colbrunn. They play a ranging set of original music that spans from dreamy psychedelic indie-rock to cutting pieces that circle around the heaviest end of shoegaze. Currently the band hopes to cut their teeth in the live circuit while recording their debut EP, planned to be out by the end of summer 2023.
