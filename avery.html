Avery

Drums

I spent my childhood in Phoenix, Arizona, immersed in a gospel music atmosphere. Raised by worship leaders, I naturally embraced their path, starting my drumming journey at the age of three. While my musical exposure primarily revolved around worship music, occasionally intertwined with AC/DC and Foo Fighters, my personal taste gradually developed and drew inspiration from other genres such as electronic music, R&B, rap, and jazz. Lately, my fascination has gravitated towards slowcore and space rock, and these genres undeniably shine through in my own musical expression.
